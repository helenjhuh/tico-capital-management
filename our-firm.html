Our Firm

Long Term Investors

  • Long term orientation with aligned incentives. We view the fund as an extended family office to compound wealth alongside philosophically aligned partners
  • We believe “time arbitrage” is a valuable competitive advantage. Our goal is superior long term returns on a 5 year basis. Tolerance for short term pain will boost long term outperformance
  • Tico Capital is built with a similar long term view this is a multi decade project, and we are setting upaccordingly. We will continually reinvest our profits into the fund and necessary resources

Continuous Improvement

  • We are efficiency nerds. Every part of our investment process is driven by a desire for superior outcomes. Slight improvements can yield outsized gains in returns, productivity, and transparency. We believe our existing process and its components are thorough but will continuously look to build a better mouse trap
  • Two way information exchange. We are always open to readings, process changes, and divergent opinions. Similarly, we will regularly update our partners on how our thoughts and processes are evolving

Performance Driven

  • We are focused, first and foremost, on generating alpha, delivering strong returns through the cycle with below market fundamental risk
  • We are investors. We are not asset gatherers. We are not fee maximizers. We have a sliding management fee that declines as AUM grows and are looking to manage capacity carefully, with our partners’ input
  • We are interested in succeeding with our partners, not at their expense
